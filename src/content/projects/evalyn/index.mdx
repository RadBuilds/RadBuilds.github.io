## The Brief

The goal of this project was to build voice agent that automate pre-screening.
Quantifiable questions — visa, experience, salary, start date, etc — parsed and surfaced to the recruiter.
Open-ended behavioral questions summarized into a candidate brief.
Recruiters get structured signal without sitting through hours of calls.

Can be integrated natively into any ATS, so recruiters rarely have to leave their ATS of choice.

## What I Built

Owned this start to finish — architecture, infrastructure, delivery, scope.

Simple scope. We kept it that way.

Serverless on AWS Lambda, Terraform for infrastructure, ElevenLabs for voice agents, Auth0 on the auth layer.

## Results

Monthly running cost for the MVP: cheaper than a lunch.

Shipped within time and budget. The hardest part wasn't the tech — it was holding the line on scope when it started to creep.
The result is a lean, secure, extensible product that does exactly what it was supposed to, nothing more.

Still running. Still cheap. Ready to scale.
